# Nvidia-overlay-black-screen-fix
Many people reported on Reddit the same problem: when you open certain videos recorded with the nvidia overlay it's open a video player crashed and you can't close it in any way, not even with alt + f4, or closing it in the taskbar, or opening a new desktop. Most of times peoples only get rid of it restarting the PC.
I've made a archive that forces the NVIDIA overlay.exe to stop through CMD, and pin it to your taskbar. So, when it happens again, just runs the shortcut and you can use your computer without having to restart it.
